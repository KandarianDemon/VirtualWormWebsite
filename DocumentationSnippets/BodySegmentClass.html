Body Segment

CyberElegans/wormbrain/MuscleSystem/Body Segment

Description

As the name suggests, the Body Segment represents a segment in the body of the worm. Its main function is to be subjected to the physics system of unity and represents a building block of the physics based biomechanical model of the worm body

Individual Body Segments are connected to form the worm body. CharacterJoints connect the segments to oneanother and by clamping the degrees of freedom with respect to motion and elasticity, bending rigidity can be introduced into the model.

Motion is then introduced by placing springs to the body segments.

Properties
